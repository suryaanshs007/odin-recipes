Description:

Odin is a big sucker for this dish; Poutine is a tale of Fries, Curds, Melting Cheese chunks and Gravy. A combination of these items creates a highly desired for dish, one the All Father would kill for.

The Origins:

Odin watched from his throne in Valhalla, as Midgardians created this dish in rural Qubec, in the late 1950s

The dish, as the All Father sees it, begins with the preparation of crispy fries via double serving. Following this, a rich beef gravy from a roux is made, fresh squeaky cheese curds are used, after which hot fries are layered and gravy is poured over top to melt (important step, it HAS to undergo gentle liquefaction.) the curds slightly.
