Godsmackingly Good Poutine.

The All Father wants his Poutine to look like this:

Description:

Odin is a big sucker for this dish; Poutine is a tale of Fries, Curds, Melting Cheese chunks and Gravy. A combination of these items creates a highly desired for, a dish the All Father would kill for.

The Origins:

Odin watched from his throne in Valhalla, as Midgardians created this dish in rural Qubec, in the late 1950s

The dish, as the All Father sees it, begins with the preparation of crispy fries via double serving. Following this, a rich beef gravy from a roux is made, fresh squeaky cheese curds are used, after which hot fries are layered and gravy is poured over top to melt (important step, it HAS to undergo gentle liquefaction.) the curds slightly.

Ingredients (Everything Odin wants you to have before you begin):

    Fries:

  • 2 lbs (1 kg) Russet potatoes, cut into 0.5 inch sticks
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Salt

    • Gravy

  • 6 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 0.25 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3-4 cups beef broth
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tbsp ketchup (optional, but of course, The All Father would love this.)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2-3 tbspcornstarch slurry (for thickening, He wants it thicc).

    • Topping:

  • 2 cups fresh cheese curds (at room temp, go beyond and you'll disintegrate at the hands of Odin).
